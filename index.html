Welcome to Leo District 306 A2

Leo District 306 A2 is one of the leading Leo District in Sri Lanka. It is sponsored by the International Association of Lions Club District 306 A2 and presently 25 Leo clubs are actively serving the community with the contribution of more than 950 Leos under the guidance of multi-talented executive officers. Activities conducted by Leo District 306 A2 include Childcare, Eldercare, Sports, Environmental, Healthcare, Leadership Development Projects and many more. A three days residential youth camp, a Leo Job Bank and PR campaigns also add up to making the Leo movement the voluntary youth service organization to be in.

Leo Multiple District 306 is divided into six Leo districts for the purpose of the district division is to make Leo club administration easy.

  • First Leo Club in Sri Lanka was formed in 1969
  • Leo District 306 formed in 1971/1972
  • Re-district into 306 A and 306 B in 1978/1979
  • Leo Multiple District 306 formed in 1984/1985
  • Re-district into 306 A, 306 B and 306 C in 1989/1990
  • Re-district again into 306 A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 and C2 in 2005/2006
