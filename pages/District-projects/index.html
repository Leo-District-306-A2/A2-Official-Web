District Projects and Events

Numero Uno

Numero Uno is the highly anticipated annual event hosted by the Leo District 306 A2, specifically designed for Council Officers and Club Presidents. This immersive program spans two days and takes the form of an exhilarating outbound training camp, complete with a thrilling hike and a memorable campfire gathering. Throughout this event, a wide range of activities is carefully curated to enhance the essential qualities of Leos, including leadership, teamwork, and decision-making skills. However, the primary objective of this initiative extends beyond individual growth. It serves as a platform for fostering stronger bonds and camaraderie among the Council Officers, Club Presidents, and other Club Executives. By facilitating meaningful interactions and shared experiences, Numero Uno promotes a sense of fellowship and unity within the Leo District, empowering its members to make a positive impact in their communities.

Sadaham Wandana

Sadaham Wandana is the highly anticipated annual Bodhi Pooja event organized by Leo District 306 A2. This auspicious gathering takes place during the first week of July and serves as the inaugural event of the Leo District. Its purpose is to invoke blessings of prosperity, success, and good fortune for the upcoming Leoistic year. The significance of this occasion cannot be overstated, as it symbolizes the beginning of a fresh chapter for the Leo District. Attendees eagerly participate in the religious ceremonies, which include the offering of prayers and devotions to the sacred Bodhi tree. The atmosphere is filled with reverence and spirituality, as Leos from various clubs come together to seek divine guidance and blessings. Sadaham Wandana serves as a unifying force, fostering a sense of community and inspiring Leos to embark on a journey of service, leadership, and personal growth throughout the year.

A2 Awurudu Uthsawaya

Leo Awrudu is a highly anticipated fellowship event organized by Leo District 306 A2 on an annual basis, coinciding with the Aurudu season in April, to joyously commemorate the spirit of the Sinhala & Tamil New Year. This vibrant festival encompasses a multitude of traditional and exhilarating Sinhala and Tamil New Year activities, creating a sense of unity and merriment among the Leos. Dressed in splendid avrudu attire, Leos gather to partake in this festivity with unwavering enthusiasm and camaraderie, fostering stronger bonds among the Leos of the District and the Leo Clubs. This celebration serves as a platform for the Leos to not only honor their cultural heritage but also embrace the values of teamwork, friendship, and community involvement. Leo Awrudu is an event that encapsulates the essence of the New Year and serves as a catalyst for the growth and togetherness of the Leo community.

A2 Sports Day

A2 Sports Day is the highly anticipated annual sporting extravaganza of Leo District 306 A2. This spectacular event aims to uncover the hidden talents in sports possessed by the Leos within the Leo District. Spanning several days, it showcases a diverse range of sports that are meticulously organized to foster and enhance the leadership qualities, sports skills, and teamwork among the Leos. The event serves as a platform for the Leos to shine and demonstrate their athletic prowess. They participate with great enthusiasm and dedication, engaging in various sports activities that not only challenge them physically but also nurture their competitive spirit. It is a celebration of their hard work, commitment, and determination. Throughout A2 Sports Day, the exceptional talents of the Leos are duly recognized and honored through awards and accolades. This recognition serves as a motivation for them to continue excelling in their chosen sports and strive for even greater achievements. A2 Sports Day truly embodies the spirit of sportsmanship, camaraderie, and personal growth within the Leo District.

Cristmassio

Every year, the dedicated A2 Leos put their creative minds together to meticulously plan and execute a magnificent event that captures the true essence of Christmas. Known as "Christmas in December," this delightful celebration has become a cherished tradition. It is an enchanting affair brimming with heartwarming carols that resonate through the air, filling the hearts of all attendees with a sense of joy and goodwill. This event serves as a powerful catalyst, fostering a strong sense of camaraderie and unity among the Leos of the Leo District 306 A2. By coming together and embracing the spirit of Christmas, these remarkable individuals not only spread cheer and merriment but also strengthen the bonds of fellowship. Christmas in December offers a blissful atmosphere where people can immerse themselves in the magic of the season, reminding everyone of the true meaning and beauty of Christmas.

Annual Leo Trip (Leo Sarisara)

The Annual Leo Trip of the Leo District 306 A2, popularly known as the Leo Sarisara, is a thrilling and much-anticipated event that takes place once a year. This exciting outing is designed as a single-day adventure, packed with activities such as hiking, and other exhilarating journeys. The primary objective behind organizing this event is to foster stronger fellowship and camaraderie among the Leos within the Leo District. It serves as a platform for Leos to connect, collaborate, and build lasting bonds through shared experiences and challenges. The Leo Sarisara encourages participants to step out of their comfort zones, pushing their limits while enjoying the breathtaking beauty of nature. With each passing year, the Leo Trip continues to unite Leos in their pursuit of adventure, personal growth, and a stronger Leo community.

Reflexion - Mid Year Review

Reflexion serves as the annual Mid-Year Review of Leo District 306 A2, offering a comprehensive assessment of the district's activities. Scheduled at the conclusion of the first half of the Leoistic year, this event aims to evaluate the progress of individual clubs within the district and the overall functioning of the District itself. During the review, club presidents provide detailed reports on their club's performance over the previous months and outline their future objectives. Simultaneously, the District Council Officers share status reports concerning their specific responsibilities. Furthermore, the event serves as a platform to inspire and motivate clubs that have shown commendable progress, with the presentation of awards and recognitions. This gathering facilitates collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and strategic planning to ensure the continued success of Leo District 306 A2.

A2 Con

A2 Con, the Annual District Conference and Awards Night of Leo District 306 A2, is undeniably the most highly anticipated event in the Leo Calendar. It represents a dazzling occasion where the relentless dedication and remarkable achievements of the Leos within the district are evaluated and celebrated. The primary objective of this grandiose affair is to acknowledge the tireless efforts of the Leos, both collectively and individually, across various projects and clubs. In this magnificent gathering, deserving winners are honored with prestigious awards and certificates, serving as a testament to their exceptional contributions. Moreover, A2 Con serves as a powerful motivator, inspiring Leos to strive for even greater heights in the forthcoming Leoistic year. This captivating event captures the essence of Leoism, fostering a spirit of camaraderie, enthusiasm, and continuous improvement among its participants.

Leo Youth Camp

Leo Youth Camp is a thrilling three-day outbound camp that offers a plethora of exciting activities, including invigorating hikes, captivating dramas, lively campfire gatherings, engaging team-building exercises, and exhilarating challenges. This meticulously organized event has a primary objective of fostering leadership qualities, enhancing individual personalities, fostering teamwork and effective communication skills, promoting out-of-the-box thinking, and uncovering the latent talents within the Leos. By participating in this transformative camp, Leos not only develop their leadership potential but also form lasting bonds of fellowship and collaboration with their peers from across the district. The Leo District places a strong emphasis on cultivating camaraderie and cooperative spirit among its members, thereby strengthening the overall unity and synergy within the district. Leo Youth Camp serves as an invaluable platform for personal growth, skill enhancement, and fostering meaningful connections that extend far beyond the duration of the camp itself.
